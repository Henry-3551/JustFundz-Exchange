Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers about accounts, security, funding, and withdrawals so you can trade with confidence.

Getting Started

How do I open a JustFundz account?

Create an account on the Register page, confirm your email, and complete your profile. Once verified, you can fund your wallet and start trading.

What markets can I trade?

Trade major crypto pairs, stock indices, commodities, and forex from one unified dashboard.

Is there a minimum deposit?

Yes. The minimum depends on the plan you choose, with entry plans designed for new investors.

Security & Funding

How do you protect my funds?

We use industry-standard encryption, device verification, and monitored withdrawals. Two-factor authentication will be available on launch.

How long do withdrawals take?

Most withdrawals are processed within 24 hours. Processing time may vary by payment method.

Can I reset my password?

Yes. Use the “Forgot password” link on the login page to request a secure reset.
